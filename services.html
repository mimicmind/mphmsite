Decision memo

When you need a call made, with reasoning you can defend.

  • Problem framing, assumptions, and information gaps
  • 2–4 options with risks and tradeoffs
  • Recommendation + execution plan
  • Appendix: calculations, sources, interview notes (if applicable)

Common uses: fleet strategy, route choices, vendor selection, incident response decisions.

Operational review

When something is drifting: cost, delays, quality, or safety.

  • Current-state map (process + incentives)
  • Failure modes and bottlenecks
  • Interventions with KPIs and owners
  • 30/60/90-day improvement plan

Common uses: turnaround plans, port-call performance, charterparty process, safety systems.

Due diligence support

When you’re underwriting a deal or partnership.

  • Commercial / operational diligence checklist
  • Red flags and confirmatory evidence
  • Management questions that bite
  • Decision memo for IC / stakeholders

Common uses: acquisitions, financing, JV partnerships, vendor/platform selection.
